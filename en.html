My vision & philosophy

Why I do what I do

At my core, I love understanding how things work. Even more when I can break them, tweak them, then make them run better than before. For me, tech isn’t just about code — it’s a tool to simplify life, unlock situations, and sometimes even save time to grab a coffee.

I’m not chasing perfection, but I do like things to be clear, smooth, and (while we’re at it) nice to look at. My approach is built on three simple ideas: keep it clean, keep it efficient, and skip the pointless headaches. If I can code a solution in half a day instead of sitting through two weeks of meetings… guess what I’ll pick.

And since all of this evolves insanely fast, I stay constantly tuned in — not because I have to, but because I actually enjoy it. Testing a new tool, exploring a new tech, or hacking an API… it’s all part of the fun.

"Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication."

Leonard — a buddy of mine (da Vinci, not the guy from the café)
